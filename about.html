Shane Temple grew up in the beautiful mountains of Northeast Tennessee.

In 2006 he volunteered to serve our country by enlisting in the US Navy. Shane spent the next 8 years as an Aviation Machinist Mate which is the Navy's way of saying Shane was an aviation mechanic. He trained on the SH-60B platform. The Navy called them Seahawks, but most are more familiar with the Army's version called a Blackhawk. He served in Jacksonville, FL, New Orleans, LA, and deployed into the waters of Central and South America in support of counter narcotics.

After his honorable discharge, Shane changed his career path to Harley-Davidson sales while studying Media Communications at Full Sail University. During his time at Adamec Harley Davidson in Jacksonville, FL his design skills were quickly discovered when he started creating several videos and animations for the dealership. This led him to founding the Band of Idiots along with 2 of his co-workers. He took all of the design tasks on himself by creating a logo and logo animation for their videos. They quickly gained traction and designed apparel items including sunglasses that were produced and shipped by Epoch Eyewear.

He became fast friends with Adam Sandoval of Scootin' America and to this day Adam is a valuable mentor and business partner of Shane's. He founded Stubborn Apparel Co and Adam was interested in taking over so that Stubborn could reach a new level, so he and Adam partnered up and Adam is currently working to build the brand with several design services being provided by Shane.

Shane has created 3D characters and animated them, he has filmed/edited real estate videos, designed several logos, edited many logo animations and video intros with special effects, created flyers/business cards/infographics/magazine ads for various businesses as well as for his education, plus so much more. After deciding to work graphic design full time, Shane relocated to his childhood home in the Tri-Cities area of Northeast Tennessee.
